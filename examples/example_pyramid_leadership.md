## Strategy
*How we will succeed*

### Our Behaviours

- We speak the language of the business, not HR jargon
- We challenge ideas respectfully and welcome being challenged
- We share data openly and explain our decisions clearly
- We experiment, learn fast, and pivot when needed
- We celebrate success loudly and learn from failure openly

### Strategic Drivers

#### Experience

Deliver exceptional employee experiences that drive engagement and performance

**What success looks like:**

> Employees say our systems are easier to use than their personal apps

> New joiners tell friends they've never had a better onboarding experience

#### Partnership

Be trusted business partners who solve complex problems, not order-takers

**What success looks like:**

> Business leaders come to us first with their biggest problems – because they trust we'll solve what others can't

> Regional MDs say we understand their local challenges better than anyone

#### Simple

Simplify processes and remove friction from everything we do

**What success looks like:**

> Line managers say they spend 50% less time on admin than a year ago

> Employees can complete any HR task in under 3 minutes

### Enablers
*What makes our strategy possible*

**Workday HRIS Platform** (System)
: Modern, cloud-based HR system replacing legacy tools

**People Analytics Capability** (Capability)
: Dedicated team and tools for data-driven insights

**Agile Operating Model** (Process)
: Cross-functional squads with empowered decision-making

## Execution

#### Deploy Workday globally
**Primary Driver:** Simple • Target: Q2 2026 • Owner: HRIS Programme Director

Complete Workday implementation across all 23 countries, replacing 7 legacy HRIS systems

*Also contributes to: Experience (30%)*

#### Launch Employee Experience Index
**Primary Driver:** Experience • Target: Q1 2026 • Owner: People Analytics Lead

Real-time dashboard tracking 10 key experience metrics, available to all employees

#### Establish Business Partner CoE
**Primary Driver:** Partnership • Target: Q3 2026 • Owner: Head of Business Partnering

Create Centre of Excellence with 5 senior business partners embedded in each region

*Also contributes to: Experience (20%)*

**Primary Driver:** Simple • Target: Q4 2026 • Owner: Employee Services Director

**Primary Driver:** Partnership • Target: Q4 2027 • Owner: People Analytics Lead

Publish 3 industry whitepapers and speak at 5 major conferences
